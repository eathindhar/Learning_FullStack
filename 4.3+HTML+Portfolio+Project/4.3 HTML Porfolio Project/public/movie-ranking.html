My Favorite Movie Selection

This list show the top 3 list of movies I have watched over the years and would recomend others to watch it.

Pannaiyarum Padminiyum

Plot: The story is single layered and revolves around a car. A village Pannaiyar or landlord (Jayaprakash) takes a liking for a Fiat Premier Padmini car that has been left in his care by a friend.
Pannaiyar falls in love with the car and appoints Murugesan (Vijay Sethupathi) a tractor driver as his personal driver. Soon the car becomes an all-purpose vehicle for the villagers. But for Pannaiyar, his wife Chellamma (Thulasi) and Murugesan, the car is a like a family member. Due to unavoidable circumstances the car is taken away leaving them heartbroken.
Director: S.U. Arun Kumar
Writer(s): S.U. Arun Kumar
Music Director: Justin Prabhakaran
Cast: Vijay Sethupathi, V. Jayaprakash, Tulasi

Imsai Arasan 23rd Pulikesi

Plot: Long after, king mukkayappan (Nagesh) giving birth the twins. Sagilimayan(Nasar) is kings personal adviser and he is younger brother of king's wife. He ask to kill one of the child to rule the kingdom. But astrologist reveals that during the crescendo period killing the new born is not good for the kingdom. He advised to loose the younger one elsewhere. One child lives in kingdom under the shadow of is uncle. Another lives with the aid mans home. They both grow in different environment. Elder brother 23 m pulikesi (Vadivelu) doing all sorts of mischief in kingdom. While elder brother uukirabuthan (Vadivelu) bought up in village. Story deals with current political suture. Uukirabuthan fights his uncle and saves his kingdom from being enslaved.
Director: Chimbudeven
Writer(s): Chimbudeven
Music Director: Sabesh-Murali
Cast: Vadivelu, Nassar

'96

Plot: Ramachandran aka Ram is a travel photographer. He visits his high school and is overcome by memories. A reunion is arranged through their school WhatsApp group. At the reunion, his friends Murali, Subhashini and Sathish catch up with him. Murali hesitantly mentions that Janaki aka Jaanu (Ram's childhood love) is coming from Singapore.
Director: C. Prem Kumar
Writers: Charu Gupta, C. Prem Kumar
Music Director: Govind Vasantha
Cast: Vijay Sethupathi, Trisha
